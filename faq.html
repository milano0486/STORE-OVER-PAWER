Frequently Asked Questions

1. What makes us different from other servers?

The difference is that we are not just a default framework, but we use unique custom scripts. Also, everything on the server is made with great passion and you can see that when you come to play with us.

2. Is the server whitelist?

Yes, the server is whitelist and you must be at least 16 years old to play (of course, there are exceptions).

3. How can I get whitelist?

You need to join our Discord and fill out a written application in the "whitelist" channel or wait for a voice interview in "Waiting Room".

4. How can I contact an admin if I have a problem?

If you have a question or need help, you can open a ticket from the "ticket" channel or join and wait for one of us in the "Waiting" channel. Please provide as much information as possible about your problem so we can resolve it more easily.

5. What jobs can I do on the server?

You have a wide choice of online jobs. There are restaurants, two car services, a hospital, police, municipality, car dealership, and pawn shop. Each of these professions is made with unique scripts you may not have seen anywhere else!

6. Where can I buy a car?

The server has two car dealerships where you can buy a car. From one you can get a Chinese car (GTA 5 vanilla car), and from the other a branded car. You also have the option to buy a car with bitcoin (real money). To buy a car with bitcoin, check the catalog of available cars and then open a ticket stating which car you want to buy, and we will explain where to send the money for the car.

7. How can I buy a house or apartment?

You can see the available houses on the map, and if there are none, you can go to the municipality and submit an application for a private property of your choice.
