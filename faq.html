Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Over Power?

Over Power is a streetwear brand that represents strength, confidence, and bold identity. We offer unique clothing designed to make you stand out.

2. How can I place an order?

You can order directly through our website. Just select your product, choose your size, and complete the checkout process.

3. Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer worldwide shipping. Shipping fees and delivery time vary depending on your location.

4. How long does delivery take?

Local orders take 2–5 business days. International orders may take 7–14 days depending on the country.

5. Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, returns and exchanges are accepted within 14 days of delivery. The item must be in original condition. Contact our support for help.

6. How can I join the Over Power team?

You can apply through our JOIN US page. We’re always looking for ambassadors, models, and creatives who believe in our message.

7. How can I contact support?

You can contact us via the Contact Us page or email us at: support@overpowerstore.com
